Project 2: Python Coded Game

Design Considerations

Overview

I will be coding a Python focused game. It is about words and basically like a Hangman program. It will be scrambled each round and you can play multiple times.

End Users

The end user of this program would be people who likes words and english in general or people that want to expand their vocabulary in a fun way. The age group that this program would be appropriate for is for aged 6+.

Requirements

What should this include:

    A list of words

    Randomiser

    Different levels of words

Specifications

A game that will showcase a whole list of words.

It should be fast and won't cause any errors when the input is different.

This would be a game that any user can play without any restrictions.


Relevant Implications

Aesthetics

To maintain the aesthetics of my character, I will continue to use the same colour pallete throughout this project and make sure that the aesthetics will also please my end-users. I would also ask feedback from my peers to gather information about improvement and what I could change to make the product look proper.

Usability

I will be testing every now and then to check whether the changes that I have made with aesthetics as well as functionality has affected the usability of my character. By doing this step, I can avoid bigger problems as I can easily keep track of what part of the process has affected the usability of my character. I will also gather feedback from my peers and ask how their experience with my character, whether the quality of my character is okay and not experiencing problems.

Functionality

To make sure that my character is functioning properly, I will ensure that the steps that I have made throughout this project will make my character function the way that my end-users want it to function. I will also make sure that my character functions properly and is not affecting any other factor outside functionality such as usability and aesthetics.

Intellectual Property

This character is inspired by a character from the game 'Mobile Legends: Bang Bang' named 'Nana' who is a sweet Leonin. My character on the other hand is a human fox. By changing features such as colours, face shape, and overall aura, I would be able to make my character different from existing characters. I would also not allow people to copy my work as it is my right to have my work protected but people can use my character as an inspiration for theirs.
